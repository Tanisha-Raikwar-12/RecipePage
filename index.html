Jalebi

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • Oil for frying
  • Sugar syrup (for soaking)

Recipe

  1. Mix flour, yogurt, turmeric, and baking soda to form a batter.
  2. Heat oil in a pan for frying.
  3. Pour the batter into a piping bag and pipe into hot oil in circular shapes.
  4. Fry until golden brown, then soak in sugar syrup.
  5. Serve hot or at room temperature.

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
